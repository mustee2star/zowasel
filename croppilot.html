ZOWASEL CROP PILOT BENEFITS

Benefits for Farmers
  • Dedicated Advisors: We offer five year tailored agronomic support to help farmers maximize the long-term value of carbon farming, increase income, and improve soil health.
  • Climate Finance: We support farmers with alternative climate finance to drive the transition to sustainable agriculture.
  • Market Access: We guarantee narket access for climate finance and market access to sell all crops cultivated under the program.
Benefits for Corporation
  • Monitor Value Chains: Monitor your footprints across value chains to meet voluntary sustainability goals.
  • EUDR Deforestation-free Compliance: Farm-to-factory traceability to monitor, report and verify your supply chain footprint to meet regulatory compliance and the EUDR Due Diligence Statement (DDS)
  • Sustainable ROI: Eliminate expensive human-led data entry and time-consuming manual calculation to access digital observatory tools anywhere and anything to save time and money.
