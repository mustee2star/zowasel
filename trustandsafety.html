Zowasel FAQ

Zowasel typically makes payment within 48 to 72 hours after the delivery confirmation by buyers. To protect our sellers and buyers, we follow the normal buyers' payment terms by making payments after delivery within 48 to 72 hours. However, some buyers' typical payment terms are up to 30 to 60 days. Hence, sellers will be informed if payment will exceed the normal term, as stated, before supply. Sellers are advised of every buyer's payment terms before offer acceptance and commodity shipment. Whatever the term, payment is guaranteed for every successful transaction on the Zowasel platform!
Buyers oversee offloading processes and provide sellers with warehouse results at no cost. Sellers are not responsible for paying to offload their trucks at the buyer's warehouse. After conducting a quality check, buyers oversee offloading processes and provide sellers with warehouse results at no cost. N.B: Some buyers require payment for offloading.
We operate an escrow account system to protect our sellers and buyers from default. To protect our sellers and buyers, even when a buyer pays ahead in full or deposit, Zowasel holds the payment in an escrow account and only makes payment to the seller as soon as the buyer confirms the waybill received per the buyer's payment terms.
Zowasel signs fulfillment agreements with buyers to protect sellers on the Marketplace platform, after which the buyer issues payment domiciliation to join the Zowasel platform. We match buyers and sellers of the same commodity. Decisions on pricing and terms are agreed on together. Zowasel then enforces smart contracts and ensures that all transactions are shipped and payments are made after delivery without issues.
We enable you to create your offers or orders to receive bids and match with trusted counterparties on the Marketplace. Instantly secure bids and enter into contracts directly with the selected sellers or buyers from our network of trusted counterparties effortlessly using our technology. We offer a fully digital experience to trade with trusted and vetted counterparties.
We engage exclusively and directly with our sellers' network to meet buyers' specifications and demands. We conduct sellers' due diligence, including site inspections for quality assurance before deliveries. We conduct a thorough site visit and inspection and get samples to test the crop quality before shipment.
We aim to create season-long relationships between growers and buyers lasting many years. From our boots-on-the-farm, Crop Test Centers and field operation expertise, we ensure the fulfillment of a successful first season. We ensure that third-party inspections justify any deductions and help match the right trading parties to maximize returns on the crop. Get introductions from our network at no cost.
We match hundreds of buyers and sellers of the same commodities, allowing them to decide on pricing and terms together. Zowasel enforces smart contracts. We match buyers and sellers of the same commodity. Our platform connects thousands of crop sellers with leading crop buyers locally and internationally. We are enabling buyers and sellers of crops to join our Preferred Network to trade with trusted counterparties securely.
For new users to start transacting on Zowasel, it depends on the verification of the users and parties. Once registered, we start due diligence and vendor verification process to ensure we match verified counterparties. You will be able to start trading right after that.
